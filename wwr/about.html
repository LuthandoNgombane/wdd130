Our mission is to provide safe, exciting whitewater adventures that connect people with nature while creating unforgettable memories. We believe in sustainable tourism and fostering a love for river ecosystems.

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiast John Riverstone, Rapid Adventures began with just two rafts and a passion for whitewater. What started as a small operation in the Colorado Rockies has grown into one of the most respected rafting companies in the West, with trips on five major rivers and a team of 30 expert guides.
