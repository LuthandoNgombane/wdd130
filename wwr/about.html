Our mission is to provide safe, exciting whitewater adventures that connect people with nature while creating unforgettable memories.

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiast John Riverstone, Rapid Adventures began with just two rafts and a passion for whitewater.

What started as a small operation in the Colorado Rockies has grown into one of the most respected rafting companies in the West.
