Overview

Purpose

Purpose: We want you to share information about our services so you may get the one of the best adventure experiences with Kayab Club Adventure. Take sometime away from the city and come experience adventure offered by mother!

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) Our target customers are lower-middle to upper-middle class income earners who work very hard and really love the outdoor sports, preferabbly between 18 to 64 years of age and reside in the cities around our business. What do they want? Our customers want to get away from the sofas and want to stretch themselves and find it pleasurable to enjoy good rafting and chill with other raft enthusiats in the evening and talk about their experiences. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)?

Access to our services will be on the web and mobile for both android and IOS.
