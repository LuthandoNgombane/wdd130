Luthando Ngombane

Luthando Ngombane is a Junior Full Stack Developer at 360 Administration and Systsems which is based in Roodepoort, South Africa. He was born in the small town in rural Eastern Cape, called "Mthatha" and moved to Gauteng in pursuit of his dream to become a Software Engineer. He only had matric and had no idea how to enter the tech field, and had to earn his living so he went into retail, retail banking and finally found his break into tech when he graduated the Hyperion Dev Full Stack Web Developer Bootcamp in the year 2023.

South Africa

Mthatha, alternatively rendered Umtata, is the main city of the King Sabata Dalindyebo Local Municipality in Eastern Cape province of South Africa and the capital of OR Tambo District Municipality. The city has an airport, previously known as the K. D. Matanzima Airport after former leader Kaiser Matanzima.
